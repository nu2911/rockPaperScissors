About

Rock Paper Scissors is a simple yet fun game that pits you against the computer in a game of chance and strategy. The rules are simple:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

Game Features:

  • Real-time Gameplay
  • Track Your Wins, Losses, and Draws
  • Responsive Design for All Devices

How to Play:

  1. Select your move: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  2. The computer will randomly select its move.
  3. The winner is determined based on the rules mentioned above.
  4. Play again or reset the scores at any time!
